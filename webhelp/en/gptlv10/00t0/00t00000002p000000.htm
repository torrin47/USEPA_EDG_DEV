How to Create an Account

To access restricted information at the EDG, or to manage content for your EPA organization, you must have a EDG account.

If you are an EPA employee or contractor with a LAN id and password, your log-in is your LAN id and password.

If you are not an EPA employee or an EPA contractor with a LAN id and password, please request information about obtaining a EDG account using the Feedback link at the top of the EDG home page.

If you need information about becoming a EDG Steward, please use the Feedback link at the top of the EDG home page, and indicate that you are interested in becoming a EDG Steward.
