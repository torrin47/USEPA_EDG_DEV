How to Provide Feedback

When the Feedback link is clicked in the upper right corner of the geoportal interface, the Feedback page loads. To provide feedback, you must enter your name, email, and comments in the fields on the feedback page. Your comments will be sent as an email to the geoportal site administrator, and a copy of the email will be sent to the address you provide. Providing feedback is useful for:

  • requesting Publisher privileges
  • sharing comments or problems
  • requesting that your username be sent to you if you have forgotten it
If you receive an error after clicking Send Mail, it is likely that security permissions do not allow sending email to the address you provided.
