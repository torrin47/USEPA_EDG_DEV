How to Integrate with the con terra security solution

The con terra security solution can provide third party components that may be useful for managing sophisticated security and license constraints, among other service and metadata features (see http://www.conterra.de/en/products/sdi/index.shtm). The geoportal can be integrated with four pieces of the con terra security solution - the securityManager, licenseManager, serviceMonitor, and smartEditor.

Note:

If you are interested in this customization, please contact the Esri SDI Solutions team (portal@esri.com) for further information and configuration.
