Using the Data Category Multi-select

The Data Category multi-select allows you to select one or more types of data to include in your advanced search query.

To select more than one entry at a time, hold down the Ctrl button on your keyboard and click on the items you would like to select in the list.

To select many items in a sequence in the list, hold down the Shift button on your keyboard and select the first and last items that span the list you would like to select.

To de-select an individual item in the list, hold down the control button and select the item in the list that you wish to remove from your selection set.
