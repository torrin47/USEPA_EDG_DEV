Related EPA and Federal Resources

EPA's National Geospatial Website
EPA's National Geospatial Program coordinates the use of geospatial data in a variety of ways to help EPA carry out its mission to protect human health and the environment.
EPA's Intranet GIS Website
EPA provides several GIS resources for Agency staff, contractors and grantees. The Intranet GIS site provides access to GIS software, data, training, policies, and much more. 
EPA's Metadata Editor Website
The EME is a simple geospatial metadata editor that allows users to create and edit records that meet the EPA Geospatial Metadata Technical Specification and Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) Content Standard for Digital Geospatial Metadata (CSDGM) requirements.
Federal Geospatial Platform Website
The Geospatial Platform provides shared and trusted geospatial data, services, and applications for use by government agencies, their partners and the public.
Federal Geographic Data Committee Website
The Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) is an interagency committee that promotes the coordinated development, use, sharing, and dissemination of geospatial data on a national basis.
Data.gov Website
The purpose of Data.gov is to increase public access to high value, machine readable datasets generated by the Executive Branch of the Federal Government.
Geo.Data.gov Website
Geospatial data comprise the majority of the data in Data.gov, and based on public feedback of the importance of place-based data and visualization, Data.gov and Geospatial One-Stop (GOS) are were integrated to become Geo.Data.gov. Make and share maps, publish data and map services, and cooperate on acquiring new data.
